Blogposts

Every week I will try to write something about my time in the USA. I may stretch the definition of a week to the maximum, but at least this is my goal.

My blogposts will be about various topics, but they will all relate to my time in the USA, my adventures at Kellogg and my travels while I am here.

Weather of Chicago

I know there are a lot of Game of Thrones fans here, but I've heard "Winter is coming" so many times the past few weeks that I'm actually starting to believe that winter in Chicago is really a thing to be afraid of. I've always liked winter and extreme weather, so I'm both looking forward to it and a little scared.

All in all, I think the weather here is enough of a hot topic that it deserves its own section on this website.
