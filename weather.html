Temperature


Winters in Chicago are apparently very cold. The daily temperature is below freezing most days in winter and sometimes the temperature gets down to below 25 degrees celcius. On the other hand, in summer it gets really warm. Days above 30 or 35 degrees celsius are not uncommon

Wind


There is always a lot of wind in Chicago. In the summer it hasn't really been that bad, but the winters especially have a lot of very cold wind from the north.

Snow


I haven't seen any but there is supposed to be a lot of snow in winter. I think I like snow, so we'll see!
